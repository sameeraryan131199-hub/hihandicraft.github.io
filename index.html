Our Story

Crafted with
Heritage & Heart

H.I Handicrafts is rooted in the rich artisan culture of Moradabad — a city celebrated globally for its extraordinary metalwork. For generations, skilled craftsmen have poured their mastery into every piece we create.

We specialize in handcrafted home décor items that blend the warmth of natural wood with the elegance of metal — creating pieces that don't just decorate a space, but define it.

Whether you're furnishing a contemporary home or seeking a meaningful gift, H.I Handicrafts brings you authenticity at its finest.

Our Process

The Art of Making

01
🎨

Design & Sketch

Every piece begins as a hand-drawn sketch. Our designers draw inspiration from Mughal motifs, natural forms, and contemporary aesthetics.

02
🔨

Material Selection

We source premium brass, iron, and sustainable hardwoods. Each material is tested for quality before it ever meets our craftsmen's hands.

03
⚒️

Hand Crafting

Skilled artisans shape, beat, carve, and assemble each piece using time-honoured techniques passed down through generations in Moradabad.

04
✨

Finish & Quality

Each item is polished, treated, and inspected thoroughly. Only pieces that meet our exacting standards leave our workshop.
